Flower Care Tips

Ensure your flowers stay fresh and vibrant with these expert care tips!

1. General Flower Care Tips

  • Watering: Most flowers require fresh, clean water. Change the water every 2-3 days and trim the stems at an angle before placing them in fresh water.
  • Sunlight: Place flowers in bright, indirect light. Avoid placing them in direct sunlight, which can cause them to wilt quickly.
  • Temperature: Keep flowers in a cool room (around 65-72°F / 18-22°C), away from drafts or heat sources.

2. Flower-Specific Care Tips

Roses

  • Re-cut the stems every 3-4 days to allow better water absorption.
  • Remove wilting petals to keep the arrangement fresh.

Tulips

  • Place in a cool spot and change the water regularly.
  • Rotate the vase occasionally as tulips tend to grow toward light.

Lilies

  • Remove pollen-laden stamens to prevent stains.
  • Trim the stems regularly to maintain water absorption.

Sunflowers

  • Keep in a well-lit area and change the water every 2-3 days.
  • Re-cut the stems to keep them hydrated.
